# Scouting Label Private Exposure Posture

## Current Decision

The Scouting Label is intentionally not part of broad public exposure in the current cycle.

## Why Deferred

1. High framing sensitivity.
2. Elevated reputational risk if misunderstood in public channels.
3. Requires tightened legal/rights/artist-protection wording before scale exposure.

## Allowed Exposure Channels (Current)

- Private briefings
- Controlled strategic conversations
- Legal and rights advisory review sessions
- Invite-only partner/investor meetings

## Not Allowed in Current Cycle

- Broad social/public campaign
- Mass outreach positioning assets
- Generic press framing without legal/ethics review

## Public Re-Entry Criteria

1. Methodology wording finalized.
2. Artist rights and compensation framing hardened.
3. Legal review complete for public-facing language.
4. Risk-control narrative validated with trusted advisors.
